Each character has unqiuque ways to move quickly, and special abilities to aid in speed and damage. There is no progress on an actual game, aside from an old scratch version that will have little correlation to the final product. The game is currently in the concept phase, where I am brainstorming and prototyping different mechanics and ideas.
